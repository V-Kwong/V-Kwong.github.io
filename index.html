Designed, assembled, and programmed an autonomous robot and a controllable guide wire transmitter. Projects


Grid

Utilizing Google's Firebase Authentication, Realtime Database and Cloud Function, Grid aims to increase mentorship relations. User profiles only consist of a word cloud of their most passionate subjects. With the Google Maps API, users will be able to view nearby users' word clouds and connect with them purely based on passion. Passion fuels passion.

Launching on the Google Play Store soon!

Moment

Allows users to draw from their own personal struggles and successes to motivate themselves on their pursuits of passion through periodic notifications and user inputs of their own experiences

Rated 4.1 stars in the Google PlayStore with over 1000 downloads!
